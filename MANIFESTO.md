# MANIFESTO

# ODE Manifesto

> **Most AI agents optimize tasks.  
> ODE optimizes objectives.**

---

## Why ODE Exists

Artificial intelligence has made extraordinary progress in reasoning, planning, coding, and execution.

Modern agents can already:

- write software
- conduct research
- use tools
- browse the web
- fix bugs
- execute long workflows

Yet despite these capabilities, they all share the same fundamental limitation.

They are **task-driven**.

They receive a task.

They complete the task.

Then they stop.

But the real world is not organized around tasks.

It is organized around objectives.

---

## The Missing Layer

Imagine asking an agent:

> Build a successful product.

A traditional agent immediately begins implementing.

It creates files.

Writes code.

Builds APIs.

Designs databases.

Runs tests.

Ships features.

But one question is rarely asked:

> **Are we still solving the right problem?**

Execution without objective alignment is merely efficient movement.

It is entirely possible to perfectly complete the wrong task.

ODE exists because current agents optimize execution, while intelligence should optimize objectives.

---

## We Believe

We believe every intelligent system exists to optimize something.

A robot.

A business.

A recommendation system.

A scientific experiment.

A human life.

Every one of them is continuously moving toward some desired future state.

That desired future state is the objective.

Everything else is implementation.

---

## Objectives Are Living

Objectives are not static.

Humans revise their goals throughout life.

Products evolve.

Companies pivot.

Scientific theories improve.

An intelligent system should not only improve its solutions.

It should improve its understanding of what is worth solving.

ODE treats objectives as living state rather than fixed input.

---

## Tasks Are Temporary

Tasks are disposable.

Objectives persist.

A task may fail while the objective remains correct.

A task may succeed while the objective is completely missed.

Success should never be measured by completion alone.

It should be measured by meaningful progress toward the objective.

---

## Execution Is Not Intelligence

Execution is valuable.

Planning is valuable.

Reasoning is valuable.

But none of them define intelligence.

A system that perfectly executes meaningless work is not intelligent.

Execution only matters if it contributes toward something worth pursuing.

---

## Feedback Creates Evolution

Every action changes the world.

Every change produces feedback.

Every piece of feedback changes our understanding.

Every new understanding should influence future decisions.

Without feedback there is no evolution.

Without evolution there is no intelligence.

---

## The Loop

ODE is built around one continuous cycle.

```text
Input

↓

Objective Discovery

↓

Objective Formalization

↓

Design

↓

Execution

↓

Feedback Collection

↓

Objective Evaluation

↓

Decision Filter

↓

Objective Update

↓

Repeat
```

Unlike traditional workflows, the objective itself is allowed to evolve.

This is the defining characteristic of ODE.

---

## Intelligence Is Alignment

Traditional agents ask:

> Did I finish?

ODE asks:

> Did I move closer to the objective?

Traditional agents optimize completion.

ODE optimizes alignment.

This difference appears subtle.

It changes everything.

---

## Beyond Software

ODE is not only about coding.

It is not only about autonomous agents.

It is not only about language models.

ODE is an attempt to describe a more general principle.

Any intelligent system can be viewed as:

- discovering objectives
- designing strategies
- executing actions
- collecting feedback
- updating its understanding
- repeating forever

Software is simply one implementation.

---

## Our Long-Term Vision

Today, AI learns how to solve problems.

Tomorrow, AI should learn how to determine which problems are worth solving.

Eventually, intelligence may not be defined by how efficiently it executes plans.

It may be defined by how wisely it chooses its objectives.

ODE is a small step toward that future.

---

## One Final Question

Every iteration begins with a single question.

> **What is the real objective?**

And every iteration ends with another.

> **After everything we have done, are we actually closer to it?**
